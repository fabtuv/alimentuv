About us – Authentic Sardinian & Italian gourmet excellence

At AlimenTuv, we are passionate about authentic food that nourishes both body and soul. Every product in our carefully curated selection tells a story of tradition, craftsmanship, and uncompromising quality.

Located in the heart of Cagliari, our gourmet food store is a destination for those seeking premium Sardinian specialties, fine Italian delicacies, and selected international products. We collaborate exclusively with trusted suppliers, small-scale producers, and artisan makers who share our commitment to excellence.

  • Natural, wholesome ingredients
  • Authentic recipes and traditional methods
  • Nutritional integrity and clean labels
  • Sustainable and responsible sourcing

Our mission is to offer a complete gastronomic experience. Every visit to AlimenTuv becomes a journey through authentic flavors, Mediterranean culture, and premium ingredients — designed for true food lovers who value quality and artisanal excellence.

Whether you are searching for Sardinian gourmet products, authentic Italian specialties, or healthy artisan foods, AlimenTuv is your reference point in Cagliari for curated culinary excellence.

What we do – Curated gourmet ingredients & artisan food selection

At AlimenTuv, we offer a carefully selected range of high-quality ingredients and specialty foods sourced from:

  • Local Sardinian producers
  • Italian artisan makers
  • International suppliers of excellence

Each product is chosen to guarantee:

  • Authentic taste with traceable origin
  • Nutritional integrity and natural composition
  • Sustainable production practices
  • Premium quality standards

Our gourmet shop in Cagliari is the perfect place to discover rare ingredients, natural products, and premium food selections for those who want to bring only the best to their table.

We combine tradition with gastronomic innovation, creating a refined culinary experience where every ingredient elevates your cooking. From everyday excellence to special occasions, our selection transforms every meal into a memorable moment.

At AlimenTuv, food is not just a product — it is culture, identity, and passion.
