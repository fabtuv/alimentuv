About Us

At AlimenTuv, we love food that nourishes both body and soul, and we believe every product tells a unique story. We carefully select the finest Sardinian specialties, authentic Italian products, and trusted international suppliers, choosing only natural, high-quality ingredients. Visiting our grocery store in Cagliari is a journey through genuine flavors and premium ingredients, discovering the best of local and Italian culinary excellence.

What We Do

We bring gourmet and everyday products of exceptional quality directly to your table, sourced from small local producers, Italian artisans, and international suppliers. Every item is thoughtfully chosen to provide a unique culinary experience, combining taste, sustainability, and authenticity. Whether you are a local or a visitor in Cagliari, you can enjoy a selection of rare, natural, and premium ingredients crafted for those who seek true flavors and exceptional products.
