Why Strawberry Tree Honey Is Bitter – And Why It’s Special

Unlike conventional sweet honey, corbezzolo honey has a distinctive bitter finish. This comes from its naturally high concentration of polyphenols and arbutin — powerful antioxidant compounds studied for their protective properties.
