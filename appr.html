Insights into Our Products

Discover the finest food specialties selected by AlimenTuv: Sardinian extra virgin olive oil, artisanal pasta, natural honey, Sardinian mullet bottarga, and gourmet ingredients.

Every product in our store is carefully selected for quality, authenticity, and origin. On this page we share the stories and characteristics behind the exceptional products we offer every day.

Sardinian Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Selected from small Sardinian olive mills, this extra virgin olive oil expresses rich aromas, superior quality, and Mediterranean tradition.

Italian Artisan Pasta

We select artisanal pasta producers using high-quality grains and traditional methods for rough, aromatic, and flavorful pasta.

Natural Artisan Honey

Selected from small beekeepers, this honey is non-pasteurized and untreated to preserve authentic aromas and natural properties.

Sardinian Mullet Bottarga

A gourmet Sardinian delicacy, rich in flavor and marine aromas, made from carefully selected mullet and traditionally cured to enhance every dish.

Umami Black Garlic

Umami black garlic is a natural superfood with a sweet and aromatic flavor, rich in antioxidants and ideal for gourmet recipes.
